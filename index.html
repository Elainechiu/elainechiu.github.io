Research

My research addresses fundamental challenges in causal inference under hidden bias. I develop exact and nonparametric sensitivity analysis methods that extend beyond binary treatments, enabling valid inference under unmeasured confounding for categorical and continuous treatment settings. My current projects explore how robustness and efficiency can be formally characterized through design sensitivity, Bahadurâ€“Rosenbaum efficiency, and related asymptotic measures.
