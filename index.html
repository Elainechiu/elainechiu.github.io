R Package: Exact Sensitivity Analysis for Categorical Data

About

My research centers on causal inference, sensitivity analysis, and hypothesis testing, with a special interest in analyzing observational studies.

Research

I develop new tools for exact, nonparametric sensitivity analysis in multi-category contingency tables. My work provides valid inference under unmeasured confounding and extends Rosenbaum’s model to general I×J tables.
