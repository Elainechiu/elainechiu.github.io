My research focuses on causal inference, sensitivity analysis, and robust statistical inference, developing exact and computationally efficient tools for observational studies with unmeasured confounding.

Research

My research develops new statistical frameworks for sensitivity analysis in observational studies. I focus on extending exact, nonparametric methods to handle multi-category discrete data and continuous data under stratified, matched-pair, or cluster-based designs. A central theme of my work is to characterize robustness through design sensitivity and Bahadur–Rosenbaum efficiency.
