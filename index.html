I am Elaine K. Chiu (also known as Elaine Chiu), a 5th-year Ph.D. student in Statistics at the Department of Statistics, University of Wisconsin–Madison, advised by Professor Hyunseung Kang. My research focuses on causal inference and observational studies, particularly on developing computationally efficient sensitivity analyses for unmeasured confounding under non-binary discrete or continuous treatments with stratified, matched-pair, or clustered designs, with applications to public health and education data.
