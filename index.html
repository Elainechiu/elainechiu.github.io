I am a 5th year Ph.D. student in Statistics at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, advised by Professor Hyunseung Kang. My research is on causal inference and observational studies, with a particular focus on sensitivity analysis for unmeasured confounding bias in observational studies. I have been developing methods for sensitivity analysis under non-binary treatment or clustered treatment. I am interested in applications to public health and education data.
